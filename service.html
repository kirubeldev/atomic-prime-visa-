Work Permit Services

Work Permits for Different Countries

Germany

Work Permit Type: EU Blue Card, General Employment Visa

Requirements: Valid job offer, professional qualification. For the EU Blue Card.

Duration: Initially granted for 1-4 years, with the possibility of extension.

Canada

Work Permit Type: Temporary Foreign Worker Program (TFWP), International Mobility Program (IMP)

Requirements: Job offer, Labor Market Impact Assessment (LMIA) or exemption for IMP.

Duration: Usually 1-2 years, with options for extension or applying for Permanent Residency.

Italy

Work Permit Type: National Employment Visa

Requirements: Job offer from an Italian employer, professional qualification.

Duration: Typically 2 years with the possibility of renewal.

Greece

Work Permit Type: National Employment Visa

Requirements: Employment offer, proof of professional qualifications, and adequate financial resources.

Duration: Issued for 1 year with possible renewal.

Belarus

Work Permit Type: Temporary Work Permit

Requirements: Job offer, valid contract, and professional qualifications.

Duration: Typically 1 year, with the possibility of renewal.

Romania

Work Permit Type: General Work Permit, Highly Skilled Worker Permit

Requirements: Job offer, proof of qualifications, valid contract.

Duration: Issued for 1-2 years with options for renewal.

Russia

Work Permit Type: Standard Work Permit, Highly Skilled Specialist (HSS) Work Permit

Requirements: Job offer from a Russian employer, professional qualifications, and salary requirements for HSS permit.

Duration: Typically 1 year, with extensions available.
